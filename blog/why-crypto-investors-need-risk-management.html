Why Crypto Investors Need Good Risk Management

By Sylvain Chassang

Investing is a bit like rock-climbing that way. Before you take big rewarding risks, it’s a good idea to have good risk management in place. Crypto is a good example.

This makes it very hard for any one to invest a serious share of their portfolio in Crypto. From an emotional perspective, it’s easy enough to HODL when you got your crypto for dirt cheap, in the early days of mining. It’s much harder when you’re investing savings accumulated over years of hard work.

Without any risk management in place, the money you invest needs to be money you can afford to lose entirely.
